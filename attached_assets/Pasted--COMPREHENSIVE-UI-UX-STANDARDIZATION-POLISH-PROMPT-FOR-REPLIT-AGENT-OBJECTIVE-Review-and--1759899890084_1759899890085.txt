**Task:** Ensure consistent spacing, padding, and margins throughout the app.

**Standards to Enforce:**
- **Section Spacing:**

  - Grid gaps: `gap-6` for main grids, `gap-4` for tighter layouts

- **Action Items:**

  - [ ] Standardize container max-widths (choose 6xl or 7xl consistently)
  - [ ] Ensure responsive spacing scales properly (base â†’ md â†’ lg â†’ xl)

#### 1.3 TYPOGRAPHY CONSISTENCY
**Task:** Standardize font sizes, weights, and line heights.

**Standards to Enforce:**
- **Headings:**
  - H1: `text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl font-bold` (hero titles)
