FAQs for Script to Video

What is Pictory’s Script-to-Video feature?

Pictory’s Script-to-Video feature allows you to transform written content into engaging videos in just a few clicks. Simply paste your script or text, and Pictory automatically generates professional-quality videos with visuals, background music, and AI voiceovers.

Can Pictory help me write a script if I don’t have one?

Yes! Pictory includes AI script generation tools to help you brainstorm and create engaging video scripts. Whether you need a YouTube video intro, a social media ad, or an educational tutorial, Pictory’s AI can draft the script for you.

Can I customize my script-to-video project?

Absolutely! Pictory lets you customize every aspect of your video:

  • Choose or upload your own footage and images

  • Select from multiple AI voiceovers or record your own

  • Add captions, brand colors, and logos

  • Edit background music and pacing

Is the Script-to-Video feature beginner-friendly?

Yes! Pictory is designed for non-video editors. You don’t need technical skills or editing experience. The AI handles the heavy lifting, and you can fine-tune the final output with easy drag-and-drop editing tools.

Can I try the Script-to-Video feature for free?

Yes, Pictory offers a free trial so you can test the Script-to-Video feature and see how quickly you can turn text into professional videos.

How does Pictory generate videos from a script?

When you paste your script into Pictory, the platform uses AI to analyze your text and match it with relevant stock footage, images, and animations. It then adds captions, voiceovers, and background music, creating a polished video that’s ready to share.

What types of videos can I make from a script?

You can use Pictory’s Script-to-Video tool for:

  • YouTube videos

  • Social media content (Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook)

  • Marketing ads and product promos

  • Training and educational videos

  • Explainers and presentations

Does Pictory add captions automatically?

Yes, every video created with Script-to-Video comes with automatically generated captions, making your content more engaging and accessible for global audiences.

How does Script-to-Video help with SEO

Videos created with Pictory are optimized for online visibility. You can repurpose your blog posts, articles, or marketing copy into videos that:

  • Increase watch time and engagement

  • Improve click-through rates on social platforms

  • Boost rankings on YouTube and Google search
